TRIO Student Support Services First-Gen Priority

Free federal program for first-gen, low-income, or disabled students. Includes academic advising, free tutoring, career exploration, tech assistance, financial aid help, TRIO scholarships, and cultural enrichment activities. If you are a first-gen student you very likely qualify - apply on the HC website.

Food 2 Finish Free

HC's food insecurity program. Free food pantries on every campus plus diapers and hygiene products. Partners with Feeding Tampa Bay and HC Foundation. No shame, no judgment - it is there for you.

Free Counseling - BayCare Partnership

HC partners with BayCare Life Management to provide FREE professional and confidential counseling for students dealing with stress, mental health, substance use, or life challenges. Contact: hcccounseling@hccfl.edu

Disability Services - OSSD

Accommodations for students with physical, mental, or learning disabilities including temporary injuries. Available on all 5 campuses. ADA compliant with ramps, elevators, accessible parking and restrooms throughout all campuses.

Step-by-Step Guide for New HC Students
1
Apply to HC - Submit the HC General Application at hcfl.edu/admissions. Rolling admissions so you can apply anytime. Summer 2026 deadline is May 26, 2026.
2
Complete your FAFSA - Go to studentaid.gov and use HC school code 007870. Do this as early as possible - financial aid priority deadlines are months before classes start.
3
Submit transcripts - Send official high school or GED transcripts plus transcripts from any previous colleges attended.
4
Set up your HC login - You will receive login credentials in your acceptance email. Use them to access MyHC Portal at my.hccfl.edu.
5
Attend orientation - All students are required to attend orientation. Visit the HC Orientation page to find the right type for you.
6
Meet with an advisor - Use HawkGPS in your MyHC Portal to plan your degree path. Advisors are available at all 5 campuses.
7
Register for classes - Fall 2026 uses the new Workday Student system. Summer 2026 uses HawkGPS. Check hcfl.edu/register-now for instructions.
8
Apply for TRIO - If you are a first-gen student apply for TRIO Student Support Services. It is free and gives you access to tutoring, advising, scholarships, and more.

Degree Types at HC
  • AA - Associate in Arts - Transfer pathway to USF, UF, FSU and all Florida state universities. Guaranteed transferability.
  • AS - Associate in Science - Career-ready degree, get a job faster in your field.
  • CCC - College Credit Certificate - Shorter program under 2 years in specific technical areas.
  • PSAV - Postsecondary Adult Vocational - Vocational training for direct employment.
  • BS - Bachelor of Science - RN to BSN Nursing program only, fully online.
Project Purpose

The HC First-Gen Navigator is an AI-powered guidance tool built specifically for first-generation college students at Hillsborough College. It helps students who are the first in their family to attend college navigate financial aid, choose the right academic program, discover free support services, and plan a realistic path to graduation - all in plain language with no jargon, in both English and Spanish.

Technical Architecture
AI Model
Claude Opus 4.6 by Anthropic - most capable model, chosen for nuanced personalized guidance
Backend
Netlify Serverless Functions proxy API calls securely - API key never exposed to browser
Database
Supabase PostgreSQL logs every conversation, feedback rating, and language preference
Hosting
Netlify with GitHub CI/CD - auto-deploys on every code push
Feedback Loop
Thumbs up/down after every response logs to Supabase for continuous improvement
Bilingual
Full English and Spanish support - student says en espanol and entire conversation switches
Agent Design
  • Role/tone: Warm, encouraging, zero jargon - like a knowledgeable friend who went to college
  • Intake logic: Always asks 2-3 questions first before giving advice so responses are personalized
  • TRIO awareness: Proactively mentions TRIO to every first-gen student since most qualify and don't know it
  • Major pipeline: Connects every major choice to the HC degree name, campus, job titles, and Tampa Bay salary ranges
  • Health science flag: Automatically flags competitive admissions for Nursing and all health science programs
  • Transfer guidance: Explains AA degree as the guaranteed transfer pathway to all Florida state universities
