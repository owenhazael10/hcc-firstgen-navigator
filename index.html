TRIO Student Support Services First-Gen Priority

Free federal program for first-gen, low-income, or disabled students. Offers academic advising, tutoring, career exploration, tech help, financial aid assistance, TRIO scholarships, and cultural activities. If you're first-gen, you likely qualify — apply on the HCC website.

Academic Success Centers (Free Tutoring)

Free tutoring on every campus, in-person and virtual. Book appointments through WCOnline or just walk in. Available in math, writing, science, and more.

Food 2 Finish Free

HCC's food insecurity program. Free food pantries on every campus, plus diapers and period products. Partners with Feeding Tampa Bay. No shame, no judgment — it's there for you.

Free Counseling

HCC partners with BayCare Life Management to provide FREE confidential counseling for students. Contact: hcccounseling@hccfl.edu

Disability Services (OSSD)

Accommodations for students with physical, mental, or learning disabilities — including temporary injuries. Available on all campuses.

1. Specialized purpose

An AI agent built specifically for first-generation college students at Hillsborough Community College. It helps students navigate financial aid deadlines, choose the right major or certificate based on their goals, understand the FAFSA process, discover free support services, and map out a realistic academic path — all in plain language, with no jargon, and in both English and Spanish.

2a. Core features
  • Role/tone: Warm, encouraging, zero jargon — like a knowledgeable friend who went to college and wants to help you succeed
  • Capabilities: HCC financial aid deadlines, FAFSA guidance, major/certificate matching, career exploration, Bright Futures info, support services, clubs, bilingual (EN/ES)
  • Limitations: HCC-specific only — not a substitute for an official HCC advisor
  • Response style: Asks intake questions first, then gives personalized guidance — not a wall of generic info
  • Agent name: HCC First-Gen Navigator (also "Hawk Guide")
